## GEOFLUID 2026: Piacenza Expo's biennial trade show

From **7 to 10 October 2026**, Piacenza Expo hosts **GEOFLUID**, the international exhibition dedicated to technologies for water, soil, and subsurface management, as well as environmental remediation. A biennial reference event for geologists, environmental engineers and hydrogeological specialists: four days of European exhibitors, thematic conferences and high-level technical sessions.

## Getting to Piacenza Expo from Milan and beyond

The fastest option is the train: **33 minutes from Milano Centrale** on the Frecciarossa, with up to **52 direct services a day**. Piacenza station is just **900 metres** from Roma284 — about a 9-minute walk. From Parma or Bologna the connection is equally straightforward. Travelling by car, the motorway exit is only a few minutes from the Expo. Full details on the [Getting Here page](/en/getting-here/).
