## Niall Horan in Milan: the date to mark

On **October 28, 2026** Niall Horan brings his "Dinner Party Live On Tour" to the stage of the **Unipol Forum in Assago**, on the outskirts of Milan. For anyone travelling from out of town, choosing where to spend the night can make the difference between an easy trip home and a stressful evening hunting for parking or public transport. Piacenza, comfortably connected to Milan by train, is a practical and quieter alternative to staying in the city itself.

## A historic centre just steps from the station

Roma284 is located in the **historic centre of Piacenza**, **900 metres from the station (about a 9-minute walk)**: convenient for anyone arriving by train who'd rather not rely on taxis or night buses after the concert. The streets of the centre stay quiet even in the evening, ideal for those getting back late after the show who just want to reach their bed without any hassle.

## Getting back without stress, even late at night

**Self check-in with a smart lock**, active from 3:00 PM, lets you get in on your own no matter what time you return from Assago, with no need to notify anyone or wait around for keys. The next day, checkout is at 10:00 AM, with everything you need close at hand: a **fully equipped kitchen** for an unhurried breakfast, a **private patio** to unwind, and **1 Gbit/s fibre WiFi** for anyone who's already back to work. Travelling with a dog or a cat is no problem either: pets are **always welcome free of charge**. Booking directly also saves you up to 15% compared to other channels.
