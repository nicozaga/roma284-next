## Why choose Piacenza as your base

Piacenza is a human-scale city, with a quiet historic centre you can explore on foot, and it stays conveniently connected to Milan by train. Staying here means avoiding the crowding of the areas near the stadium on big event days, while still enjoying the evening without compromises. At Roma284, in the heart of the historic centre, you'll find a private patio to unwind after the concert, a fully equipped kitchen for those who prefer a quiet meal before heading out, and 1 Gbit/s fibre WiFi to work or stay connected. The apartment is pet-friendly, so travelling with your pet doesn't mean arranging things elsewhere.
