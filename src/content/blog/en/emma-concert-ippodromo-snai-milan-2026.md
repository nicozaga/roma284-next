## Emma at Ippodromo Snai San Siro: the date to mark

On **September 9, 2026** Emma takes the stage at **Ippodromo Snai San Siro** in Milan for a stop on her 2026 outdoor tour. For those coming from out of town, the practical question is always the same: where to stay without stress and without spending a fortune in central Milan. A convenient base can be found in **Piacenza**, comfortably connected to Milan by train, with the station just **900 metres (about a 9-minute walk)** from Roma284.

## Free check-in, no schedule to follow

Anyone arriving in the evening after the concert knows how valuable it is not to have to coordinate keys with anyone. At Roma284, **self check-in with a smart lock** is active from **3:00 PM**: arrive whenever you like, open the door with a code, and get settled independently, even late at night after the last encore. Check-out is at **10:00 AM**, convenient too for those who prefer to leave at a relaxed pace the day after the event.

## Why base yourself in Piacenza

Beyond the flexibility of entry, the apartment offers a **private patio** to unwind before or after the evening, a **fully equipped kitchen** for those who'd rather not eat out every time, and **1 Gbit/s fibre WiFi** for anyone working or wanting to stream concert clips without a hitch. All in a **quiet historic centre**, away from the buzz of event nights. For those arriving by car, the A1 stop is also convenient; for those travelling with pets, they're **always welcome free of charge** at Roma284. Booking directly, you can also save up to 15% compared to other channels. The guide on [how to get here](/en/getting-here/) is also useful for planning the trip to Milan.
